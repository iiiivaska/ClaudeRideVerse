# TraVerse — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 22, 2026

## Overview

TraVerse is a cycling and outdoor activity tracker with a fog-of-war exploration mechanic. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

### Location Data
- GPS coordinates are collected **only while you are actively recording a ride**
- Location data is stored **locally on your device only**
- We do not transmit your location to any server

### Motion Data
- Motion sensor data may be used to detect when you stop moving (auto-pause feature)
- This data is processed on-device and not stored or transmitted

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone)
- We do not use analytics or tracking services
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not use advertising
- We have no user accounts or cloud storage

## Data Storage

All ride data (routes, hex exploration progress, statistics) is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. No data leaves your device.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:
- **Delete** any ride data within the app
- **Uninstall** the app to remove all data completely

## Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted here.
